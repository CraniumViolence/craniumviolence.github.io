# Mirage League Challenges
This guide makes the assumption that you have a baseline understanding of Path of Exile and does not include detailed descriptions of all core mechanics or systems.

Mechanics related to the current league or new mechanics in general will naturally be covered more in depth.

I was full doomer after awful breach replaced good breach but Mirage is looking a lot better. I will still miss classic breachstones, they were very comfy.

Also I just want the Burger King set. It's awful, tacky crap in a very endearing way.

[.semismol]Reminder that you should never buy supporter packs before you actually play and like the league!

# 9 Added Accessories
<div id="ch-9" markdown="9">
## Easy
<h2 class="req">3 of 6 required</h2>
[.ggg]Complete any three of the following League Mechanic encounters the specified number of times.
- [.ggg]Defeat Monsters with Essence (0/30)
	- [.com]Atlas points can guarantee at least **1** monster each map
	- [.com]`Essence Scarab` adds **2** monsters up to a max of **10** while using five
	- [.com]`Essence Scarab of Calcification` converts **all rare monsters** in a map to be imprisoned
- [.ggg]Open Strongboxes (0/30)
	- [.com]Atlas points can guarantee at least **1** strongbox each map
	- [.com]`Ambush Scarab` will add **4** to a map up to a max of **12** while using three
- [.ggg]Activate Shrines (0/30)
	- [.com]Atlas points can guarantee at least **1** shrine a map
	- [.com]`Domination Scarab` adds **2** shrines to a map up to a max of **8** while using four
	- [.com]`Domination Scarab of Apparitions` adds **2** Apparition Shrines to a map
	- [.com]`Domination Scarab of Evolution` adds **1** Evolving Shrine up to a maximum of **2** while using two
- [.ggg]Defeat Possessed Monsters (0/30)
	- [.com]Atlas points can add at least **1** Tormented Spirit
		- [.com]Pairing this with `Rogue Exiles` is sensible as `Exiled Will` causes Rogue Exiles to be possessed
	- [.com]`Torment Scarab of Possession` grants a fixed 25% chance for each rare in an area to be possessed up to a maximum of 75%
	- [.com]`Torment Scarab` adds **3** additional Tormented Sprits to an area
	- [.com]Do not take `Speaker of the Dead` if you want to complete this
	- [.com]This is an obvious skip because it's awkward compared to the rest
- [.ggg]Defeat Rogue Exiles (0/30)
	- [.com]Atlas points can add **1** Rogue Exile and can cause them to appear in pairs or spawn extras with a map boss
	- [.com]`Anarchy Scarab` adds **4** Rogue Exiles** up to a maximum of **20** while using five
	- [.com]`Anarchy Scarab of Partnership` is a 50% chance for Rogue Exiles to appear in pairs
- [.ggg]Defeat Rare Beyond Monsters (0/30)
	- [.com]A `Beyond Scarab` and the Atlas can all enable Beyond monsters spawning
	- [.com]Atlas points can lower requirements for spawning monsters and `Endless Tide` makes it so you cannot spawn bosses (bosses stop further beyond monster spawns by default)
	- [.com]`Beyond Scarab of Haemophilia` and `Beyond Scarab of the Invasion` both make spawning Beyond monsters easier
	
Some of the numbers above for scarabs might be wrong as they were adjusted to make up for the removed map device and I might have missed totals.
</div>

# 16 Deadly Deeds
<div id="ch-16" markdown="16">
## Normal
<h2 class="req">5 of 11 required</h2>
[.ggg]Complete any five of the following encounters while the area level is 81 or higher if possible the specified number of times.
- [.ggg]Abyssal Depths (0/5)
	- [.com]There's a new `Abyss Scarab of Descending` that forces Depths spawns but it might not be cheap
- [.ggg]Logbooks with at least 10 Remnants active (0/5)
	- [.com]You can simply buy these and then it's as simple as playing them normally
	- [.com]These are just the modifiers that activate as you blow things up
- [.ggg]Tier 4 Harvest Seeds (0/5)
	- [.com]Crop Rotation can get you Tier 4s relatively even if it's not completely consistent
	- [.com]The `Harvest Scarab of Cornucopia` exists and allows you to force at least one Tier 4 monster to spawn
- [.ggg]Domains of Timeless Conflict with at least 25 rewards (0/3)
	- [.com]Can be done in a four or five-way fairly easily as long as your build is decent at clearing, simply get the emblems
	- [.com]Buying in to a five-way rota will obviously complete this for you while also levelling you but is a lot more than simply buying the emblems if your character can handle it
	- [.com]This might sound hard if you are unfamiliar with the content, but it is legitimately not even a single full row of rewards and it does not take a notable build to reach this amount
- [.ggg]Blueprints with 4 revealed wings (0/5)
	- [.com]Requires engaging with Heist as all the blueprint will require that you have levelled up your Rogues to be taken
	- [.com][Very simple to search for on the trade website](https://www.pathofexile.com/trade/search/Mercenaries/lVlbq2psV), simply look for area level of 81 and set the heist section to include four revealed wings
		- [.com]The in game async trading search should presumably also cover this.
- [.ggg]Simulacrum Waves (0/60)
	- [.com]Simply waves, doesn't need you to complete them
	- [.com]I would avoid attrition grinding if you can't do at least 10 waves and do something else instead as you will ultimately just be wasting money otherwise
- [.ggg]Fully Completed Ultimatums (0/10)
	- [.com]This is a full set of 10 or more waves depending on your atlas tree, not too bad but a lot of builds will struggle with T14 and full ten waves
- [.ggg]Blighted or Blight Ravaged Maps (0/5)	- [.com]Can be cleared on any build even with zero DPS because of how the systems work
	- [.com]`Clear Oil`, `Sepia Oil` and `Amber Oil` can `Slow Monsters`, `Increase Tower Damage` and `Make Towers Cheaper` respectively
		- [.com]`Blighted` maps allow for up to three of any one type of oil while `Blight Ravaged` allow for up to three of three types of oil
		- [.com]You can mix and match oils, you don't need to use only one type
	- [.com]Ring anoint for Meteor Burning Ground and Chilling Towers Freeze Enemies
		- [.com]`Silver Oil` and `Opalescent Oil` for `Your Chilling Towers freeze enemies for 0.2 seconds while they are affected by chilling beams`
		- [.com]`Violet Oil` and `Indigo  Oil` for `Your Meteor Towers create Burning Ground for 3 seconds on Hit`
		- [.com]You can also go with `Azure Oil` and `Sepia Oil` to get `Your Empowering Towers have 25% increased Effect` as a cheaper alternative to the freezing anoint (not recommended if you want to try Blight Ravaged without exceptionally high DPS)
	- [.com]Leave Seismic, Empowering and Chilling Towers at Level 3, use them in choke points and to stop things reaching the pump in general
	- [.com]Minions should go to Scout Towers, Fire should go to Meteor Towers
	- [.com]Build multiple damage types in each lane to cover immunities and try to build towers around actual lane chokepoints
	- [.com]Avoid immune to stun, chance to avoid elemental ailments and cannot be slowed below base speed as modifiers
	- [.com]Blight Ravaged is a lot harder but can still be managed with just those two ring anoints if you don't get a really, really bad layout
- [.ggg]All Rituals in Maps with at least one Blood-filled Vessel applied (0/10)	- [.com]Needs Ritual Vessels made from 100x Ritual Splinters
	- [.com]Use them on completed rituals to fill them up
	- [.com]Use filled ones as a map fragment when opening a portal to add the monsters to rituals
	- [.com]This is a fairly big undertaking compared to some of the others, you cannot fill vessels from rituals that used vessels making this a lot more than 20 in most cases
- [.ggg]Claim rewards from Rare Maps successfully completed by your Atlas Runners (0/30)
	- [.com]Basic kingsmarch interaction, map runners are purely a gold sink you want highest level possible for
	- [.com]It is rare maps only so you can transmute and regal maps for highest clear chances and even annul them if you're feeling crazy
- [.ggg]Complete Hive Fortresses or Defeat Breach Marshals (0/10)
	- [.com]Stack unstable breaches, disable hives and run the `Breach Scarab of the Marashal` and you can do this in two to three maps
	
A copy of last league with a token breach addition.
</div>

- Level 100 is something you probably already know if you will get or not but you can get the `Omen of Amelioration` to avoid losing XP while working towards it

# Challenges to Skip
You get the full armour set at **36/40** which allows you to skip up to four different challenges.

Obvious contenders in vague order of consideration are as follows:
- Grueling Game Grinds
	- GGG is as has been the case for most recent leagues rather tame this time around but if you don't want to run a bunch of invitations or pinnacles this is a solid skip choice

Honestly, everything is looking very easy in general. Ubers and maybe some Mirage content are the only contenders for being remotely hard or even expensive.

# Unlocks
This league is another full armour set with 36 completed challenges required to unlock all four pieces. There's various tiers to the pieces that allows you to toggle the amount of blueman group you're feeling.
- At 12 challenge completion you can unlock the Orb Regalia Boots.
- At 20 challenge completion you can unlock the Orb Regalia Gloves.
- At 28 challenge completion you can unlock the Orb Regalia Body Armour.
- At 36 challenge completion you can unlock the Orb Regalia Helmet.

You can view [a full preview video is available on the official Path of Exile YouTube channel](https://youtu.be/60p4WENX9ig) for the rewards.
