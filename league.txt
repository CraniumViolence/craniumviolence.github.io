# Unlocks
This league is another full armour set with 36 completed challenges required to unlock all four pieces. The unlock order is presumed based on the previous league. There's various tiers to the pieces that allows you to toggle the amount of blueman group you're feeling.
- At 12 challenge completion you can unlock the Orb Regalia Boots.
- At 20 challenge completion you can unlock the Orb Regalia Gloves.
- At 28 challenge completion you can unlock the Orb Regalia Body Armour.
- At 36 challenge completion you can unlock the Orb Regalia Helmet.
A full [preview video is available on the official Path of Exile Youtube channel](https://youtu.be/60p4WENX9ig).
