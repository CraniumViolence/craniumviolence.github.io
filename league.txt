# Allflame League Challenges
#### Introduction
This page covers all of the challenges for the **Curse of the Allflame** league. 

While the guide makes the assumption that you have a baseline understanding of Path of Exile, it will still give fairly detailed information on even the simpler challenges.

Difficulties are subjective and are oriented around thoughts on doing them in a trade league, SSF is always going to be notably harder. If you are trying to complete challenges and this is your first league following a build guide is probably wise.

Mechanics related to the current league or new mechanics in general will naturally be covered more in depth.

[.semismol]Reminder that you should never buy supporter packs before you actually play the league and find it's worth supporting!
