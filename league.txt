# Mirage League Challenges
This guide makes the assumption that you have a baseline understanding of Path of Exile and does not include detailed descriptions of all core mechanics or systems.

Mechanics related to the current league or new mechanics in general will naturally be covered more in depth.

I was full doomer after awful breach replaced good breach but Mirage is looking a lot better. I will still miss classic breachstones, they were very comfy.

Also I just want the Burger King set. It's awful, tacky crap in a very endearing way.

# Unlocks
This league is another full armour set with 36 completed challenges required to unlock all four pieces. There's various tiers to the pieces that allows you to toggle the amount of blueman group you're feeling.
- At 12 challenge completion you can unlock the Orb Regalia Boots.
- At 20 challenge completion you can unlock the Orb Regalia Gloves.
- At 28 challenge completion you can unlock the Orb Regalia Body Armour.
- At 36 challenge completion you can unlock the Orb Regalia Helmet.

You can view [a full preview video is available on the official Path of Exile YouTube channel](https://youtu.be/60p4WENX9ig) for the rewards.
